About Us

History

Established in 2078 B.S., our foundation laid the groundwork for a collective vision among BIT students, culminating in the formation of our esteemed committee. Though our official documentation followed in due course, the appointment of executive members was grounded in the common interests and aspirations shared by our student body.

Present

Fast forward to the present, where our dedicated committee stands as a beacon of service, committed to a two-year tenure of excellence. With meticulous planning, we are poised to embark on the democratic process of electing our next board of members. Each member of our board exemplifies mastery in their respective domains, passionately steering our endeavors towards academic enrichment and fostering an optimal IT environment on campus. Our unwavering dedication resonates in our ongoing efforts to enhance educational standards and IT infrastructure, ensuring a vibrant learning ecosystem for all. Together, we strive to uplift the educational landscape and empower the future leaders of tomorrow.

What we do

Our steadfast commitment echoes through our continuous endeavors to elevate educational standards and bolster IT infrastructure, fostering a dynamic learning environment accessible to all. United in purpose, we endeavor to enrich the educational landscape and nurture the leaders of tomorrow. We extend our support by providing comprehensive study materials including notes, syllabus, and past questions, while also addressing any inquiries within our IT faculty. Your contributions and collaborations are invaluable to us. Additionally, we organize workshops designed to cultivate the skills necessary to excel in the evolving IT landscape, empowering individuals to thrive as developers in tomorrow's IT world. Through active engagement and the cultivation of meaningful relationships, we aim to strengthen our community and pave the path to success together.
